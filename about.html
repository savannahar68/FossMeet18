About COEP.

Engineering colleges all around the world nurture and train their students to be pioneers, inventers and achievers in life. But very few colleges have spent hundreds of years doing it. College of Engineering, Pune is one of the oldest and premiere engineering institution in the country. The institute has a rich history and dedication to the pursuit of excellence. Chartered in 1854, COEP has many eminent and outstanding alumni, Bharat Ratna Sir M. Visvesvaraya, Prof. Thomas Kailath and Dr. C. K. N. Patel to name a few. It is ranked amongst the top 20 technical colleges in India. The college offers a unique learning experience across a spectrum of academic and social experiences. It is known not only for its technical achievements but also its co-curricular ones. With its strong history and foundation, COEP continues to stimulate an environment of support and enthusiasm in young minds.

About Department of Computer Engineering & IT

The Department of Computer Engineering and Information Technology, established in 1992, offers two undergraduate programmes, namely, B.Tech (Computer Engineering) and B.Tech (Information Technology), and a post graduate programme M.Tech (Computer Engineering).The department is a recognized research center under Savitribai Phule Pune University for the Ph. D. (Computer Engineering) programme. The faculty of the department is actively engaged in research in the areas of Cloud Computing, Information Security, Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Data Analytics, Bio-informatics, Networking, Green Computing, Signal Processing, Multimedia and Embedded Systems. The department promotes open source software development. The department boasts of having strong alumni base and industry association. For more details Click Here

About COFSUG(COEP's Free Software Users Group )

COEP's Free Software Users Group (COFSUG) is a group of volunteers to promote the use of Free Software in COEP and in other institutions in Pune. The group runs a mailing list cofsug@googlegroups.com. The group carries out activities like: Installation fests (to teach people how to install GNU/Linux operating system), Workshops on Linux Administration, Drupal, Python, Various FOSS technologies, Creative Commons, Open Hardware and like. The group promotes the development of software with release of code under GNU GPL-v3 license. Join us at :- COFSUG Google group

About COEP - ICSRG(COEP's - Information and Cyber Security Research Group)

COEP's - Information and Cyber Security Research Group (COEP - ICSRG) is formed by the volunteers to do novel research in Information and Cyber Security in COEP and other Institutes. The group members acquire knowledge in current state of art systems and develop systems using open source softwares or freewares. The group shares knowledge to society and create awareness through conducting seminar, workshop, symposium, conference and media.
